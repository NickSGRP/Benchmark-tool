# Dream Firm Benchmark Analysis Tool - User Guide

Welcome to the Strategic Group Benchmark Analysis Tool. This guide will help you understand and use each feature of the tool to assess your firm's performance across 20 key industry metrics.

## Getting Started

The tool measures your firm's performance across four critical domains:
- **People** - How effectively you convert talent into sustainable value
- **Process** - How reliably and efficiently work moves through your firm
- **Data** - How trustworthy and usable your information is
- **Technology** - How systems and automation remove friction

Your data is saved automatically in your browser and will be available when you return.

---

## Tab 1: Details & Assumptions

**Purpose:** Understand what each benchmark measures and why it matters.

**How to use:**
1. Review each of the four domains (People, Process, Data, Technology)
2. Read the description to understand what the domain measures
3. Note the "Owns" section - what the domain controls
4. Note the "Outcome" section - the results you should expect
5. Review all 5 metrics within each domain to understand:
   - What each metric measures
   - The typical (average) performance level
   - The high-performer benchmark target

**Key information:**
- **Typical** = Industry average performance
- **High** = Top performer benchmark (your target)
- Metrics use different units: percentages, dollars, days, counts, or a 0-2 scale

---

## Tab 2: Input

**Purpose:** Enter your firm's actual performance data across all 20 metrics.

**How to use:**
1. **Enter Firm/User ID** - Your name or firm identifier
2. **Select Date** - The date of this assessment
3. **Work through each domain:**
   - Start with "People" (selected by default)
   - Fill in all 5 metrics for that domain
   - Click "Continue to [Next Domain]" button
   - Repeat for Process, Data, and Technology

4. **Progress tracking:**
   - Watch the progress bar (X/20 completed)
   - You must complete all 20 metrics before saving
   - The tool won't let you save incomplete data

5. **Saving:**
   - Once all 20 metrics are complete, click "Save Complete Response"
   - Your data is saved in your browser automatically

**Tips:**
- For **numeric metrics**: Enter exact numbers (e.g., staff turnover rate as 12.5 for 12.5%)
- For **scale metrics** (0-2): Select from the dropdown:
  - 0 = Below benchmark
  - 1 = At benchmark  
  - 2 = Above benchmark
- The "Target" shown next to each field is the typical benchmark

---

## Tab 3: Analysis

**Purpose:** See detailed performance analysis of your most recent submission.

**What you'll see:**
1. **Summary cards** - Overall performance by domain (shown as % above/below benchmark)
   - Green (+) = Above benchmark
   - Red (-) = Below benchmark

2. **Domain Details** - Breakdown of each metric within a domain:
   - Your actual value vs. the benchmark
   - Visual progress bar showing performance
   - Gap percentage (how far above/below benchmark)

3. **Export button** - Download your analysis as a CSV file for further analysis

**How to interpret:**
- Look for domains with negative scores - these need attention
- Within each domain, identify specific metrics dragging performance down
- Prioritize improvements in areas furthest below benchmark

---

## Tab 4: History

**Purpose:** View all your saved benchmark submissions over time.

**What you'll see:**
- Chronological list of all submissions
- Each entry shows:
  - Submission number and firm/user ID
  - Date of submission
  - Performance scores for all four domains

**How to use:**
- Track your progress over time
- Compare different periods to see improvement
- Export individual submissions or all data as CSV
- Use "Export All" to get a complete historical dataset

**Use cases:**
- Monthly or quarterly tracking
- Before/after comparison when implementing changes
- Multi-firm comparison (if tracking multiple organizations)

## Tab 5: Dashboard

**Purpose:** Executive-level visualization and comparison across all your data.

### View Options:
- **All Firms** - Aggregate view of all submissions
- **Individual** - Focus on a specific firm
- **Compare** - Compare selected firm against others

### Chart 1: Domain Performance (Radar Chart)

**What it shows:** Overall performance across all four domains on a single visual.

**How to read:**
- The larger the shape, the better the performance
- Each point represents one domain
- Hover over points to see detailed breakdown of metrics
- In "Compare" mode, you'll see your firm vs. peer average (dashed line)

**Use it to:** Quickly identify which domains are strong vs. weak

---

### Chart 2: Distribution (Box Plot Style)

**What it shows:** Where your performance sits relative to all other submissions.

**How to read:**
- Gray box = middle 50% of all submissions (25th-75th percentile)
- Vertical line = median performance
- Red line = benchmark (zero point)
- Green dot = your score
- Hover to see Min, Max, Quartiles, and your exact position

**Use it to:** 
- Understand if you're ahead or behind peers
- See how much room for improvement exists
- Identify if you're in top quartile, average, or need significant work

---

### Chart 3: Domain Maturity vs Performance

**What it shows:** Relationship between capability (maturity) and results (performance) for each domain.

**The Four Quadrants:**
- **Leading** (top-right) = High capability delivering results ✅ *This is your goal*
- **Fragile** (top-left) = Delivering results despite low maturity ⚠️ *Unsustainable*
- **Under-utilised** (bottom-right) = Have capability but not using it 💤 *Wasted potential*
- **Foundational** (bottom-left) = Needs investment in both 🔧 *Priority area*

**How to read:**
- Each colored dot is one domain (People, Process, Data, Technology)
- Hover over dots to see which metrics contribute to positioning
- Arrows point right (maturity) and up (performance)

**Use it to:**
- Prioritize where to invest (Foundational areas)
- Identify unsustainable practices (Fragile)
- Find quick wins (Under-utilised - you already have the tools!)

---

### Chart 4: Heatmap

**What it shows:** Detailed metric-by-metric comparison across recent submissions.

**How to read:**
- Each cell shows one metric for one submission
- Color intensity = how far above/below benchmark
  - Green = above benchmark
  - Red = below benchmark
  - Darker = further from benchmark
- Hover over cells for exact values

**Use it to:**
- Spot patterns across submissions
- Identify consistently weak metrics
- See if problems are getting better or worse

## Troubleshooting

**My data disappeared:**
- Data is stored in your browser's localStorage
- Clearing browser data will delete your submissions
- Always export important data as backup

**Can I access this on mobile?**
- Yes, the tool is fully responsive
- However, charts are easier to read on desktop/tablet

**Can multiple people use this?**
- Yes, but each browser/device has separate data
- For team-wide tracking, have everyone enter their firm name consistently
- Export and consolidate data if needed

**How do I reset everything?**
- Clear your browser's cache and cookies for this site
- Or open in incognito mode for a fresh start
